About Us

KC Constructions is owned by the KC group of Companies, a conglomerate involved in the manufacture of food, hydropower, insurance, education , Construction and banking sector. KC group established Himshree Foods in 1981. Additionally, in 1998, KC Group also founded Machhapuchchhre Bank, the first commercial bank of Nepal based in the western region of the country. Almost two decades later, the group further entered the insurance business in 2017 having major stake of Sun Nepal Life Insurance.Starting from 2005, KC Group has made extensive investments in the hydropower sector.

KC Group

In the initial time KC Construction Firm was established with the continuous effort of Kriti Bahadur KC and his son Surya Bahadur KC strive to establish more companies and KC Group of Companies become a large company ranging from Rara AAHA Noodles, Construction companies, hydro power companies, hospitality sector, education sector, banking sector, insurance sector and many other companies are on pipeline.

At present Surya Bahadur KC is the chairman of KC Group of companies and under the chairmanship of Surya Bahadur KC, his sons Prakash KC, Kiran KC and Roshan KC are actively involved to enhance the group of companies.

KC Group has been serving its services to the Nepal and Nepali since 1973. KC group has been one of the leading organizational groups of Nepal. KC group manufactures the first noodles (RARA) of the nation which is also the market trend setter for the instant noodles within the SAARC nations.

KC Group founded the first regional commercial bank of the western region of Nepal, now this commercial bank covers different regions of Nepal.

KC Group has been successful to write their success stories in the development of the hydro power projects. There are different hydropower projects which are operating smoothly with a goodoutput.

KC Group is also an active participant in educating the intermediate level and the post intermediate level graduates with the different engineering courses and also tourism and hotel management courses.

Likewise, KC Group is also very responsible towards it society. In order to maintain the healthy green environment while operating the food industry, hydro power, construction, etc. KC Group organizes and encourages the plantation programs at-least twice a year. Motivating the football players; KC Group organizes an annual event named AahaRara Gold Cup which is held in Pokhara, Nepal.

KC group started working in the Hydropower since 2005. Since then KC group have been creating a success stories in the hydropower projects, therefore they are willingly interested in working in different hydropower projects in coming days.
